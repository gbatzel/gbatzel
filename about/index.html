About

I am interested in understanding the genetic basis for phenotypic traits in marine organisms. My undergraduate research was focused on identifying calcification genes in larvae and juveniles of tube-building polychaetes. I discovered carbonic anhydrases are uniquely expressed in glandular regions of tubeworms. More recently I spent a summer at Friday Harbor Laboratories where I conducted research on the ability of urchin larvae to swim under low pH conditions.
